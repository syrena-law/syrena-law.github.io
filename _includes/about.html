About

Karen Hoffmann is an attorney in solo practice at Syrena Law in Old City, Philadelphia. She works with Aldea - The People's Justice Center to represent asylum seekers in detention, including families detained at the Berks family prison and separated at the border. She also works with the Community Environmental Legal Defense Fund to represent communities across Pennsylvania who are fighting to protect their environment. Prior to attending law school at Temple University, she worked as a journalist in Latin America.
